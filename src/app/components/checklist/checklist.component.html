Our Checklist

In our expert eyes, the way the car looks and feels speaks volumes about of how the car was maintained.

# DESCRIPTION
1 We procure cars that are customer driven and do not deal with middlemen.
2 We check the service history and ensure that the car has been serviced through authorized service centers only.
3 We do a thorough check for any impacts.
4 We ensure that the odometer is not tampered with.
5 Using our expertise we compare the tyre condition with the odometer reading.
6 We compare the paint job to the age of the car.
