ABOUT US

At a time when they were only Ambassadors and Fiats, Mr. Rahman, a well known name in the used car dealership business dealt with the supply of imported cars and ran a successful garage to fuel his passion for automobiles. His hard work and simplicity earned him a lot of respect and helped forge valuable customer relationships.

After working in a corporate job for 12 years, his son Ashfaq TP - a car fanatic himself - established Gear2Gear, a car dealership company in Bengaluru, to carry on with the family legacy and cater to a growing demand for good used cars. His knowledge in cars along with his corporate experience has helped Gear2Gear build a successful client base in India.
